## ⚡ What is AnomX?

**AnomX** is a simple Android application designed for personal safety. In emergency scenarios, it gives you instant access to life-saving tools like a shake-to-alert feature and live GPS tracking.

## 🛡️ Core Features

| Feature | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **🚀 One-Tap SOS** | Push the `EMERGENCY SOS` button to send your real-time Google Maps location to your emergency contacts via WhatsApp or SMS. |
| **📳 Shake to Alert** | Shake your phone twice rapidly to send an SOS message without needing to unlock your screen. |
| **📍 Live Location Tracking** | A real-time map on your dashboard shows your exact location so you always know where you are. |
| **📞 Emergency Numbers** | A built-in list of national emergency services that you can call instantly. |

## ⚡ Extra Features

You can turn on extra safety features from the Advanced Features dashboard:

- **🔋 Low Battery SOS Ping:**  
  *Status:* 🟢 Active  
  Automatically sends a final SOS message with your location if your phone battery drops very low, letting your contacts know your phone is about to turn off.

- **📡 Live GPS Auto-Updates:**  
  *Status:* 🔴 In Development  
  Once an SOS is triggered, the app will keep sending your new location to your emergency contacts on a continuous interval.

## 📱 How to Setup

It is very easy to start using AnomX:

1. **Add Emergency Contacts:** Open the app and enter the phone number of a trusted friend or family member.
2. **Choose Mode:** Select either `SMS Mode` or `WhatsApp`.
3. **Turn on Shake to Alert:** Toggle `Shake to Alert` to ON.
4. **Trigger an SOS:** Press the EMERGENCY SOS button or shake your phone twice to call for help.

### Technical Requirements
* **API Target:** Built for Android 12+ 
* **Core Permissions:**  
  - 📍 `ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION`  
  - 💬 `SEND_SMS`  
  - 🌐 `INTERNET`
